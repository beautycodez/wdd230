Overview

Purpose

Bountiful food is small company selling organic, and locally grown foods. It offers delivery service from Los Angeles to San Diego in trucks; that company contacts with customer by phone and the website. Also, Bountiful Food caters to walk in traffic at their storefront. The website will inform about the products of the company (organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags), promote the healthy eating, fresh food options, and set a means of contacting customers. Additionally, the website will show organic bounty and convenience.

Audience

The first customer is a person who cares for their health. That person eats healthy food and lives in the locality. the second person is a foreigner who is visiting the California coast and wants to eat healthy and fresh food
