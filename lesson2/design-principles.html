Fitt's Law

Mercado Libre

https://www.mercadolibre.com.pe/

The principle of Fitt's law is applied on Mercado Libre's website because we can see the searching bar is very visible and we can easily fullfill the purpose of the website looking for our products. We also can see the promotions in a hero banner to make it easy for customer to buy online good promotions.

Rules of Thirds

Walmart

https://www.walmart.com/

The principle of rules of thirds is applied on Walmart's website because we can see the drop down menu in the left-top of the screen; just below that we can see the second part most important, the promotions. In the mid top of the screen and to the right we have the cart and the searching bar.
