Overview

Purpose

This is a website to share interesting information about the use and/or acquisition of jewels in order to encourage the purchase and acquisition of jewels. In addition, additionally show the real models that the company has.

Audience

The website is aimed at an economically stable public, with an age range of 30 and over.

Content

Home page

The main page will be used as a summary of everything new in terms of products, and content of our blog. On this page we will have a brief section showing that we will address issues related to jewelry, tips on how to keep jewelry in good condition, curiosities related to them such as that each month has a precious stone or that silver is antibacterial. Always relevant information in order to encourage the public to acquire their own jewel or to give to their loved ones. On this page we will have a brief section showing that we will have a section where we will address and share with the public a brief review about us, how we started in the world of jewelry at the hands of our patriarch Don Abraham Alzamora who transferred the knowledge of jewelry to their descendants forming a family of artisan jewelers, we will also share how the company started, information on how to contact us. Always showing, in the images that accompany the page, the products offered by the company between necklaces, rings, earrings, and more elements created by the master craftsman jeweler.
