Audience

My target people are business man or woman who are starting or have already created a company. They are between 25 and 60 years old. Their incomes are moderate and their company is growing up slowly; However, if they don't improve the company, they may have loosings in the near future. They have finished their school and have basic knowledge about businesses. They will use cellphones or laptops to get access into the website.
The second kind of person is a successful business man or woman with a very profitable company. This person has university studies and has many contacts. He looks for setting more relationships with different companies to make deals that can benefit both sides.

Scenarios

  • How can I know more about the benefits of being a member of the Chamber Commerce?
  • I need to talk with members of the Chamber Commerce to arrange a meeting and make businesses.
  • Can I get financial education in the Chamber Commerce?
  • I need to know what activities the Chamber of Commerce will do next.
  • How can I become a member of the Chamber of Commerce?
